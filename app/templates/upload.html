Upload your equipment sensor data file (CSV format) to begin analyzing.

File Requirements

Your CSV file should contain columns such as:

  • Air temperature [K]: Environmental air temperature
  • Process temperature [K]: Operating process temperature
  • Rotational speed [rpm]: Equipment rotational speed
  • Torque [Nm]: Applied torque
  • Tool wear [min]: Tool wear measurement

Note: The system will try to match your column names automatically.
